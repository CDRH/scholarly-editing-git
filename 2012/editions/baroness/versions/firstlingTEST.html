Textual Notes

Draft clusters of this poetic event may be found on microfilm and in three folders in the Elsa von Freytag-Loringhoven papers archived within Special Collections at the University of Maryland, College Park.

  • Firstling (Erstling, He): circa 1923-1924; Reel 4, Frames 54-65; series 3, box 2, folder 3
  • Liebe; Sonnsang: undated; Reel 4, Frame 472; series 3, box 2, folder 67
  • Lilac Old (Alter Flieder); He; Pfingsten: undated; Reel 4, Frames 479 and 481; series 3, box 2, folder 68
  • Pfingst (Fanfare, Pfingstjubel, Pfingsten; Erstling; Ritter in Gold Verzieter Rüstung): circa 1923-1924; Reel 5, Frame 244-246, series 3, box 3, folder 23
For more information, please see the Finding Aid at http://hdl.handle.net/1903.1/1501.

DJUNA - I SEND YOU THIS - YOU WILL SCARCELY RECOGNIZE IT - THE ENGLISH WAS SUCH MISSTEP THAT ONLY CAN HAPPEN HERE - IN GERMANY WHERE I AM FLUSTERED FRUSTRATED - BY PROFOUND SCARE - UNREST. WITH ALMOST SUPERHUMAN LABOR - MISERY - I FIXED IT - FOR I WAS ASHAMED - ANXIOUS. I CAN NOT LAST THUS LONG - DJUNA! AND YET - I HAVE NO MESSAGE - YET! A POEM LIKD THAT - THOUGH FAULTLESS NOW - SHOULD NOT COST ME SO DEAR! THE GERMAN - FIRST VERSION - ALWAYS WAS TOUCHING PATHETIC NAIV - FINISHED. WHAT IS INTERRESTING
Alternate spelling: INTERESTING
 ABOUT THE 2 TOGETHER- IS THEIR VAST DIFFERENCE OF EMOTION - TIME KNOWLEDGE - PAIN. THAT IS - WHY THEY SHOULD BE PRINTED TOGETHER.
FOR THEY ARE 1 + 2 THE SAME POEM - PERSON - SENTIMENT LIFE STRETCH BETWEEN ONE - DIVIDED - ASSEMBLED - DISSEMBLED. THE GERMAN ONE RIPE - EXPERIENCED - BITTER. THE GERMAN ONE IS RAW DEEP WOE OF CHILD - IN WHOMS VERY VERY VIOLENCE THUS NAIVE EXPRESSED - LINGERS BALM OF RECOVERY SENSIBLE - THE ENGLISH ONE - AS IS SUPERFLUOUS TO POINT OUT - IS GRIM - SOPHISTICATED.
YES DJUNA - IT IS THAT "WOEFULL WART" POOR DEAR SUFFERING FRIEND! I COULD BURST OUT LAUGHING - IF IT WERE NOT SO TRULY FIGURATIVE IN ITS VERY DISTORTION! FOR - THAT NOW - HERE - IS MY HEART. IT IS INDECENT TO BEHOLD.
HOW IS SWEET DEAR LITTLE SARAH FREEMAN?
SHE DID NOT WRITE. OH! KISS HER.
