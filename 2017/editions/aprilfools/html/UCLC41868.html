Mary Fiske to Samuel L. Clemens
31 March 1884 • New York, N.Y.
(MS: CU-MARK, UCLC 41868)

91 Fifth Avenue
March 31st

Dear Mr Twain

I know when you see my autograph you wont refuse me yours—

I would like as many as 160 at least, being about to tend a Booth in a Church Fair

Very Truly Yours

Mary Fiske

Persons Mentioned

Mary Fiske  (1842–1889)

Mary Fiske, a Hartford native, wrote plays and drama criticism under the nom de plume "Giddy Gusher." She married the journalist and dramatist Stephen Fiske in 1875.
